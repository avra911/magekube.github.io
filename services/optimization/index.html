Your Magento Website is Too Slow, Then Fix It Faster!

No online customers bother browsing a slow website even if your content is amazingly interesting.

Meanwhile, the lightning-fast page load speed leverages guest engagement, retention, and ultimately boosts sales.

Then, what an ideal page speed should be?

Based on our experiences and several studies, the ideal loading time is average between 2 to 3 seconds, but we want to go beyond that and we usually set our goal for less than 1 second. The slower the load speed is, the higher the bounce rate will become, which is over 50% when the loading is about 5 seconds.

So, what can we help to boost your load speed?

Our dedicated team will perform code audit and performance review using both testing tools and our experiences to pinpoint the root problems of slow loading. Then, we will put forward cost-effective resolutions to leverage your web speed toward the ideal value. This process can involve using extensions, custom development, or changes in the site configuration.
